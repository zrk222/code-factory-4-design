# Trust Engineering — playbook (checkout & decision points)
Design trust in; don't bolt it on. Place signals exactly where hesitation spikes.
- Radical price transparency: total incl. fees/taxes upfront (prevents shock-factor abandonment).
- Security cues: padlock, "secure checkout", verified badges beside the CTA.
- Social proof: star ratings + review counts near primary buttons.
- Guest checkout: forced accounts cause ~26% abandonment.
- Guarantees & clear refund/cancellation policies, easy to find.
- Marketplaces: dual-blind reviews (hidden until both submit) keep proof honest.
- Reassuring micro-copy near inputs: "secure", "verified", "clean", "guaranteed".
- 89% of customers switch after one poor experience — a broken cue is expensive.
