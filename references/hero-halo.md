# Hero & the 50ms Halo — playbook
Users judge credibility in ~50ms from visuals alone; a professional hero makes
them assume the whole service is expert-led (Halo Effect), cutting bounce.
- One bold `<h1>`, one primary CTA, high-res real photography (`object-fit:cover`).
- Minimalist header = confidence (the "Apple standard").
- Airbnb: professional photos booked 24% more, commanded ~26% price premium.
- Imagery lets users *mentally simulate* the experience → lower emotional friction.
